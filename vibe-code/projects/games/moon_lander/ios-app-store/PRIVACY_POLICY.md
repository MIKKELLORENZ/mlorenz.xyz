# Moon Lander - Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** January 2025

## Overview

Moon Lander ("the App") is a game developed by Mikkel Vind Lorenz. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our App.

## Information We Collect

**We do not collect any personal information.**

The App:
- Does NOT collect personal data
- Does NOT require an account or registration
- Does NOT track your location
- Does NOT access your contacts, photos, or other device data
- Does NOT use analytics or tracking services
- Does NOT display advertisements

## Local Storage

The App stores the following data **locally on your device only**:
- Your high score
- Game preferences (if applicable)

This data:
- Never leaves your device
- Is not transmitted to any server
- Is not shared with any third party
- Can be deleted by uninstalling the App

## Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with any third-party services, SDKs, or analytics platforms.

## Children's Privacy

The App is suitable for all ages and does not collect any information from anyone, including children under 13.
